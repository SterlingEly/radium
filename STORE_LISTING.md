# Radium 2 — Store Listing

## Name
Radium 2

## Short Description
Radial bar graph watchface. Hours and minutes as arcs, battery and steps on the outer ring.

## Full Description

A watchface built around a single idea: display time as a radial bar graph.

The hour hand becomes an arc. The minute hand becomes an arc. Together they radiate outward from the center of the watch face, making the time readable at a glance from across a room — or more importantly, across a meeting table without anyone noticing you're checking the time.

The outer ring tracks two things silently in the background: battery level sweeps the right half, step count sweeps the left. Both fill from the bottom, so a full ring means a full battery and a great day on your feet.

Tap to reveal an overlay with exact numbers — date, battery percentage, and step count — then tap again (or shake, depending on your setting) to dismiss it.

**Radium 2** is a complete rebuild of the original Radium watchface (designed December 2015, published December 2016) for all modern Pebble platforms, with a new 24-preset color system and improved layout across rect and round displays.

---

**Features**
- Radial arc display for hours and minutes
- 24 color presets in three rows: Dark, Light, Color
- Outer ring: battery (right half) and step count (left half)
- Overlay: date, battery %, step count — on shake, tap, or always
- Supports all rect and round Pebble platforms
- B&W invert mode for classic Pebble and Pebble 2

**Platforms:** Pebble Classic/Steel (aplite), Pebble Time/Steel (basalt), Pebble Time Round (chalk), Pebble 2 (diorite), Pebble Time 2 (emery), Pebble 2 Duo (flint)
